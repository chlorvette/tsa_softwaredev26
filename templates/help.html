Getting Started

Follow these basics to get comfortable with the site.

  1. Sign in and confirm you can see your username in the center of the navigation bar.
  2. Browse available courses on the My Courses page and open a course to view the lessons within it.
  3. Change settings such as font size and dark mode with the controls on the Settings page.

Shortcuts

  • Use the top navigation bar to jump between Home, My Courses, Awards, Help, and Settings.
  • On the My Courses page, use the Start Course link to open a course, and the Complete Course button to finish it.
  • Inside a course, use the sidebar to hop directly to a specific lesson.

Troubleshooting

  • Refresh the page if content gets stuck or fails to load.
  • Try another browser or clear your cache if videos or images do not appear.
  • If progress is not saving, ensure you are signed in and your internet connection is stable.
  • Need more help? Email support@example.com with a brief description of the issue.
